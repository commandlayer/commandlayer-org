What gets checked

Receipts are not trusted by claim. They are verified by proof.

VerifyAgent checks the receipt structure, canonical payload hash, signature, signer records, and capability verb before returning a result.

Backed by CLAS Trust Verification

VerifyAgent validates canonical CLAS Trust Verification receipts using bundled JSON Schemas, then checks canonical hash, Ed25519 signature, signer identity, and trust verb.
