VerifyAgent.eth

Paste a CommandLayer receipt and verify schema integrity, canonical hash matching, signer identity, and Ed25519 signature validity.

VerifyAgent resolves signer keys from ENS TXT records. For this demo, runtime.commandlayer.eth is supported through the same record structure using key id vC4WbcNoq2znSCiQ.

What gets checked

Receipts are not trusted by claim. They are verified by proof.

VerifyAgent checks the receipt structure, canonical payload hash, signature, signer records, and capability verb before returning a result.
