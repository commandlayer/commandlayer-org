How to read this page

The Manifest supports strategy, naming discipline, and long-range semantic planning. It does not outrank the published Commons and Commercial contract pages or the docs hub.

  • Live: backed by published schemas you can integrate against now.
  • In flight: names with active product or protocol exploration, not stable contracts yet.
  • Reserved: protected namespace only. Useful for planning, not for shipping integrations.
  • Builder rule: Commons and Commercial define the contract. Runtime defines execution.

Live surface vs strategic namespace

CommandLayer needs both discipline and ambition. The live protocol surface remains intentionally small and sharp. The Manifest shows the larger semantic map without letting exploratory categories masquerade as shipped protocol contracts.

What is live now

Commons and Commercial published schemas are the current builder surface. They are versioned, pinned, and portable.

What this page is for

Namespace reservation, category framing, and strategic clarity about where the semantic moat can grow over time.

What this page is not

It is not a claim that every reserved verb is implemented, supported by Runtime, or ready for integrations.
