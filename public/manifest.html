How to read the Manifest



Each entry is a potential canonical verb — a unit of machine intent that can be
bound to schemas, Agent Cards, and Runtime guarantees:



  • Type: Commons (public goods) or Commercial (economic flows).

  • Category: AI, commerce, operations, finance, comms, or system.

  • Status: Live, in flight, or reserved for future standardization.

  • ENS: the semantic handle (e.g. summarizeagent.eth).
