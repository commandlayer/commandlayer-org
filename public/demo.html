What these demos prove

Both paths teach the same model. Commons is the canonical contract layer. Commercial is the commerce-oriented extension. Agent Cards define routing and identity. Runtime executes the flow and may attach proof, trace, and orchestration metadata around the receipt.

  • Commons demo proves canonical contract execution and verification.
  • Commerce extension demo proves multi-step paid execution with runtime-backed orchestration.
  • Neither path blurs wrapper fields into the canonical receipt itself.
