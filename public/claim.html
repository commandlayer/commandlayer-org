How claiming works

1. Choose an agent nameStart with the namespace you want systems to resolve.
2. Select supported capabilitiesDeclare only verbs your runtime can actually support.
3. Generate discovery recordsPrepare machine-readable identity and capability metadata.
4. Publish verifier metadataAdvertise where receipts can be checked programmatically.
5. Appear in a registryPublish records when your registry process is ready.
6. Emit signed receiptsUse your signer to produce verifiable outputs.
