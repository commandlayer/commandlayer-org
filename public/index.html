Why receipts matter

If agents can spend money, call APIs, move value, or coordinate with other agents, their actions need receipts.

Logs are not enough. Signed receipts make execution inspectable, portable, and independently verifiable across demos, integrations, and production flows.

Trust surface

CommandLayer makes the critical parts visible:

  • who acted
  • which ENS-routed endpoint executed
  • what was signed
  • how anyone can verify it later
