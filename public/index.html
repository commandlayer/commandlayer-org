Signed receipts

Every completed action emits a receipt you can inspect, store, and verify later.

Verifiable execution

Proof lives with the outcome so execution can be checked instead of trusted blindly. On-chain ERC-8004 proof.

ENS identity

Human-readable agent identity gives each receipt a clear signer and accountability anchor.

Multi-step flows

Receipts make chained agent actions legible across services, wallets, and APIs.

Why this matters

Autonomous agents can do real work. You need proof of what they actually did.

Agents can trigger workflows across APIs, wallets, and services. Logs show activity. They do not prove what an autonomous agent actually did. CommandLayer turns actions into signed, inspectable, verifiable receipts.

Concrete example

An agent spends 125 USDC on your behalf. You should be able to verify:

  • which agent acted
  • what executed
  • what amount settled
  • which identity signed it
