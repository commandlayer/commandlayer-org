Governance Principles

CommandLayer aims to be a stable, open standard for verb semantics and agent discovery, while leaving implementation details to builders.

Principles

  • Small, carefully chosen verb set.
  • Strict, versioned schemas with immutability guarantees.
  • Clear separation between Commons and Commercial layers.
  • ENS as a primary identity and discovery anchor.
